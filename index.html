Executive Summary

Risk Manager's Bottom Line

At the current asking price of $330,000, net proceeds of approximately $74,729 comfortably exceed the expected closing cost of $59,736 — leaving a $14,993 buffer. The property can be reduced to $315,000 before any savings are needed, and to $310,000 before exceeding the $5,000 savings tolerance.

The primary risk is not the price reduction itself, but closing cost uncertainty. Nine estimates spanning $45k–$75k represent a $30,000 range of uncertainty. The strategic recommendation is to resolve this estimate variance before accepting any offer below $320,000.
